How to Commission Clover Coin Designs

This can be a helpful and personal guide to getting to know the artist behind Clover Coin Designs. A helpful tool to make your commission experience even better. If you still have questions or concerns that have not been answered below, please do not hesitate to contact me! There is no such thing as a stupid question. Thank you again for your interest and support of my work!

A Quick Guide:

  1. Get to know AJ. (Make sure this artist style is what you want, if there are multiple styles by the same artist please feel free to point out example work if you want something similar. READ THE TOS!)
  2. Know what you want. (Commission type, style ideas, character choices…ect.)
  3. Contact AJ using this commission inquiry form. Discuss commission order.
  4. Send payment.
  5. AJ draws your commission!
  6. (Optional) AJ mails your commission or product!

Step 1: Be familiar with the artist you’re working with.

When you commission me, or any artist for that matter, it is very important to understand we all have different styles and artistic tastes. Always review the portfolio! As an artist, I am constantly experimenting with my skills and artwork. If you see a specific style you liked of my work and would like something similar, please let me know! I can not guess or assume exactly what you want unless you let me know.

Please be aware of my policies and Terms of Service. There are a lot of things you should be aware when it comes to using your commissioned artwork, how payments and refunds work, what can and won’t be drawn by the artist. I do my best to answer and cover all questions, but if you have more to ask please message me.

Step 2: Know what you want.

Plan ahead of time if you’re going to order from an artist. Keep a notebook of ideas you’d like to see from what artist. What characters you want to see drawn in whose style. This is very helpful when you see flash sales or quick slots. You don’t always need the full details, I am more than willing to help you out best that I can with brainstorming your order if you need it. But most importantly have a good idea on what you want for subject matter, this is what matters the most in the completion of the project. And be sure to let me know what kind of order you’re looking to get, sketches, line art, full artwork, or even just some icons.

Step 3: Talk to me! I don’t bite!

Well, unless you ask me to. I bet that would make for a fun picture. This is where it starts to get fun, all you have to do now is use the commission form provided here! Just tell me your name, contact information, references, and a rough description of the commission you have in mind and we can discuss further.

If you ever need help brainstorming a character development or commission idea, I do keep a work-only skype ready for instant messenger use. I can chat with you real time and help you come up with your next brilliant picture! I love working with the customers and helping with creative flow. If you don’t need any help and know exactly what you want, that’s no problem at all! Gets to the drawing part much more quickly.

Step 4: Sending payment.

As I’ve mentioned in my Terms of Service, I will not draw until I receive payment. If the commission order is $30 USD or below I require an upfront payment. If the commission turns out to be larger and more costly, I require a $30 USD down payment to begin. Once I have finished the order and shown you a watermarked version for your approval, you will send me the rest of the payment. Once I have received it I will send you the unmarked version of your commission.

I do prefer all payments be sent through Paypal. I will also accept cash payments at conventions or in person. The longer you wait to send payment, the longer it will take to get started on your commission.

Step 5: AJ gets drawing!

This is where I get to have some fun, if you purchased an small order like a chibi or icon from me I will take your references and complete the artwork to get back to you as soon as possible. If you do end up purchasing something a bit larger I will be happy to show a sketch upon request for approval. Edits and adjustments all should be made in the sketch stages. Minor alterations can be made in the line art. Once I begin coloring no more edits can be made to the base of the picture. I do expect you the customer to give me some artistic liberties with each project, I will do my best to make sure it fits the description you have on your order.

I use all artwork as portfolio references and do post them online for others to see. If you would like to keep your order private there will be an extra fee.

Step 6: (Optional) AJ mails it out!

If you purchased a physical art product such as prints, posters, or button set… I will package each individual order with care as needed. I ship things out Mondays – Fridays. All packages will be insured for the actual cost of the commission order. If you would like to skip insurance fees please contact us to let us know, but please be warned that it is then no longer our responsibility should the package get lost or damaged.

And that is how you commission AJ from Clover Coin Designs! Thank you so much for taking the time to read through the TOS and this little guide. Again, if you still have more questions or concerns please shoot me a message and I will get back to you as soon as I am able!

Clover Coin Commission Price Guide

Hey everyone, this is just a quick reference sheet for my prices for you all to go off of. These are all just rough estimates based on the fact that I work at $20 USD an hour. ALL PRICES DO VARY PER PROJECT! You will never know the for sure price of your commission until I see your order and it’s details are discussed in full. I will give you the final price on your project.

I do require all a $30 USD down payment on all larger commissions before beginning. All small commission orders will not be reviewed by the customer as it is too time consuming. Please make sure my art style is what you’re looking for. You will allow me artist freedoms and liberties to experiment with your character and order if it is under $30 USD.
